What are the rules?
We will have 2 tournaments that happen simultaneously:
  • One tournament for all levels
  • One for novices
Each tournament has:
  • World Cup-style format
  • Starting with a group stage
  • Teams of 3 (can be up to 5 players, you’ll need to alternate if more than 3 players) people
  • Every team will play at least 3 matches against other teams within their group
  • To win a match, a team needs to win 2 out of 3 games (each player on the team plays a game).
  • Games are to 7 points in the group stage and to 11 in the knockout stage.
  • The top 16 teams will then advance to the single-elimination knockout stage
  • One team is crowned champion!
If you can’t find a team, we can match you with one!
